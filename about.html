About This Guide

Downtown Charleston Coffee Shops is an independent local directory built by people who live here and drink a lot of coffee. No corporate backing, no pay-to-play listings, no affiliate commissions — just an honest guide to the best places to get a cup on the Charleston peninsula.

"We built the guide we wished existed when we moved here."

Charleston's coffee scene has grown fast over the last few years. New shops open, old favorites evolve, and keeping track of what's worth your time isn't always easy — especially if you're visiting and only have a morning or two to spend. Our goal is simple: give you a reliable, up-to-date starting point so you spend less time searching and more time drinking good coffee.

Every listing is based on firsthand visits and community knowledge. We focus exclusively on downtown and the lower peninsula — King Street, the French Quarter, Harleston Village, Cannonborough, and the surrounding neighborhoods. We update the directory regularly as new spots open or hours change.

Have a shop you think deserves a spot? We're always looking for new recommendations from locals who know the scene.
